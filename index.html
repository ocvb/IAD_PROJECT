About Us

In higher education in various countries, such as Canada, Nigeria and the United States, a course is a unit of teaching that typically lasts one academic term, is led by one or more instructors, and has a fixed roster of students. A course usually covers an individual subject.

Innovate Training's Short Courses provide opportunities for adult learners to acquire knowledge and skills for professional development and explore new interests for personal enrichment.

We offer wide range of career oriented and Opensource courses in collaboration with Red Hat and IIT Mumbai. Visit us for free councelling, we will help you to choose the right course. We also provide Job guidance on our Certification courses.
